About Me

Islamic Scholar & Digital Content Creator

I am Yasir Panakkad, an Islamic scholar dedicated to spreading authentic Islamic knowledge and guidance to the global Muslim community. With a deep understanding of Islamic jurisprudence and theology, I aim to make religious education accessible to all.

Through my YouTube channel, AL YASR MEDIA, and Instagram profile, I create engaging content including educational videos, motivational reels, and live sessions that reach millions of followers worldwide.

My approach combines traditional Islamic scholarship with modern communication methods to address contemporary issues faced by Muslims today.
